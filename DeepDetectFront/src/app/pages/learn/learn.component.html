Understanding Deepfake Technology

Deepfake technology uses artificial intelligence to create realistic but fake videos, images, and audio. This technology has significant implications for media authenticity and digital security.

How Do Deepfakes Work?

Deepfakes use advanced AI models called Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to analyze and recreate facial patterns, expressions, and movements.
